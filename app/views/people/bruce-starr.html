From his obituary:

Bruce Starr died October in Palm Springs, California of AIDS-related lymphoma. He was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on March 12, 1956. He was the son of Sidney Starr and Maxine Gross Starr. He graduated from Delhass High School, Levittown, Pennsylvania in 1974. In 1977, he moved to Los Angeles. In 1983, he received his Licensed Vocational Nursing degree from Casa Loma College, Los Angeles.

In 1987, he moved to San Francisco. He worked as an LVN for 13 years at various San Francisco hospitals. In 1994, he was Mr. December for the San Francisco Bare Chest calendar. He also performed in the Men Behind Bars revue with Gail Wilson from the City Swing Big Band troupe. He volunteered for the Names Project.
